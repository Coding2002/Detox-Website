ABOUT US

Therapy is considered to be a taboo subject in our society as most of the people around lack mental health awareness. Taking professional help in such situations might seem embarrassing as no one acknowledges the importance of proper mental health disorder treatment. We are all so concerned about "What will people say?" that sometimes we forget to ask ourselves what we really want.

To help you get through this while maintaining your privacy, we have designed this platform. It will securely connect you to a trained and understanding volunteer who will listen to you and guide you through your problems.
